Facts about Venus:

  • Venus is the hottest Planet (Though it's the second furthest from the Sun!)
    • Its unbearable temperature is thanks to its thick layer of clouds
  • It's about the same size as Earth
  • Venus is the brightest planet when observed from Earth
    • Check out that picture above...or better yet, go outside and see it for yourself!
  • A Venutian day is actually longer than a Venutian year! (243 Earth days vs 225 Earth days)
    • That means it takes longer to spin one time than it takes for it to orbit the sun!
