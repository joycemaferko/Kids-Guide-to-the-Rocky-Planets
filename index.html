Welcome to the kids' guide to the solar system! These pages contain information about the first four planets of the solar system: the rocky planets. You may also see these planets called "terrestrial" planets. That's another word for rocky, as opposed to the four "gas giants" which lie further away from the sun. Take a look at the pages for each of the rocky planets and learn about earth and its rocky neighbors.

Wondering if it's a beach day on Mars? Check out the most recently available temperatures at Mars' equator, courtesy of Nasa's Insight lander.
