Facts about Mars:

  • Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun
  • It's noticably red in Color
    • This reddish color comes from rusted iron in the soil
  • Mars has two moons:
    • Phobos
    • Deimos
  • Home of Olympus Mons (The tallest mountain in the solar system!)
  • The length of a day on Mars is about the same as one on Earth...
  • But a year is almost twice as long. (687 days!)
