Facts about Mercury:

  • Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun (But not the hottest!)
  • It is the smallest Planet
  • Mercury speeds around the sun but rotates very slowly on its axis.
    • One year on Mercury is only 88 earth days
    • One day, on the other hand, is 59 earth days long
    • That means one day on Mercury is nearly as long one year!
